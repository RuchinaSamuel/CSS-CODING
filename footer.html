ABOUT MYSELF

My name is Ruth Oluebube Samuel, my friends call me Ruchina Samuel. i am from Anambra State in Nnewi North Local Government area. with my parent Mr and Mrs Samuel Okonkwo.I am the last of ten (10) children, three boys and seven girls. i was born 16th of August, in early 19th century.

I started my primary education at God's time Nursary and primary school where i got my primary school leaving certificate, and part of my secondary at Maria Regina Model comprehensive school.Maria Regina is an all girls school, i was a day student because my parent never liked the idea of leaving within the school premises. my days in secondary school, was fun, competitive and inspiring. my friends, Favour and Ebube. Favour was one of our best student and Ebube was a musician then even at her young age, she had a band "Gospel kids" with her younger siblings. my days at Maria Regina ended December 2011 when i was told by my parents to go live with my uncle at Lagos.

Earlier 2012, i moved to live with my uncle at Lagos, where i continued my senior secondary education at Hope Bay College Maza-Maza Lagos.i finished my secondary school 2015, i was made the head girl of my school and this gave me the oportunity to represent my school in differnt occasions like the "Lagos experience" held in Festac 2013.
